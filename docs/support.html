Common Questions

How do I scan a product?

Open the Scan tab and point your camera at a product barcode. Hold steady for a moment and the app will automatically detect it. Make sure you're in a well-lit area for best results.

Why does the app say "No ingredient data found"?

ThyraScan uses the Open Food Facts database to look up products. Not all products are in the database yet, especially store-brand or regional items. If you know the ingredient list, you can check it manually using our ingredient reference.

How do I restore my subscription?

On the Paywall screen, tap Restore Purchase at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone to cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Profile tab within the app and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all scan history.

I found an ingredient that should be flagged. How do I report it?

Email us at safescansystems@gmail.com with the ingredient name and why you believe it should be flagged, along with any sources. We review all submissions carefully.
