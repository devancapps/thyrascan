Privacy Policy

ThyraScan — Last updated: March 23, 2026

ThyraScan ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by SafeScan Systems. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the ThyraScan mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Email address and, if you sign in with Apple, an anonymized Apple ID. We do not collect your full name unless you provide it.
  • Scan history: Product barcodes you scan and the resulting ingredient analysis are stored in your personal account to power the History feature. This data is associated with your account and stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Subscription status: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous user identifier and transaction records necessary to validate your subscription. We do not receive your full payment information.
  • Device information: Basic device and OS version information may be collected by our third-party services for crash reporting and analytics purposes.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Health records, medical history, diagnoses, or lab values
  • Symptoms, medications, or treatment information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or images (the camera is used only for real-time barcode scanning; no images are stored)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core features (barcode scanning, ingredient analysis, scan history)
  • To manage your account and subscription status
  • To improve the accuracy of our ingredient database
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

ThyraScan uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) — stores account credentials and scan history securely. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscription validation. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Open Food Facts — a public, open-source food product database queried by barcode. No personal information is sent to Open Food Facts. Open Food Facts Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — handles all in-app purchase billing. Apple Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Your scan history and account data are retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen within the App. Upon deletion, your account credentials and scan history are permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase's built-in security rules to protect your data. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

ThyraScan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account directly from the App (Profile → Delete Account). For other requests, contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
